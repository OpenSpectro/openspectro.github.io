3D Spectroscopic Profiling for Biomedical Analysis

Abstract

Our paper presents OpenSpectro, a novel open-source platform for 3D spectroscopic analysis of physiological biomarkers. We introduce a method for non-invasive health monitoring through advanced optical spectroscopy and multi-wavelength photoplethysmography, enabling real-time biomarker detection with 92% accuracy in clinical trials...
